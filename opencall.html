Open Call

Synthetica and Alterica: Silicon Valley International Contemporary Art Review Exhibition

ArtX Gallery is excited to announce the open call for Synthetica and Alterica: Silicon Valley International Contemporary Art Review Exhibition. Located in Silicon Valley, this annual gathering brings international artists to examine art and technology intersections.

Synthetica examines the fusion of human creativity with artificial intelligence, blurring the boundaries between reality and virtuality.

Alterica explores otherness, challenging perceptions of difference.

Digital Impressions - Silicon Valley International Art Exhibit

The term "impression" historically references fleeting moments captured by masters like Monet and Degas. This exhibition explores how artists harness algorithms, pixels, and code to create works echoing impressionist spontaneity and emotion in the digital age. These artworks capture our technology-driven era through unique digital expressions reflecting contemporary consciousness.

/imagine...Digital Soul - Silicon Valley International Contemporary Art Exhibition

Presented jointly by ArtX Gallery and the Gen AI Summit, this exhibition addresses whether digital entities possess "soul." Curator and artist MetaCher invites audiences to explore this concept as technology advances toward simulating human emotions and cognition, raising questions about future human-digital interaction.
