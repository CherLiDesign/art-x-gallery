About

ArtX Gallery

ArtX Gallery is an art gallery in Silicon Valley and Metaverse Voxel.

ArtX Gallery believes in the symbiotic relationship between art and technology. Planted in the center of technological innovation, 'X' symbolizes infinite possibilities, interdisciplinary collaboration, and personal immersive exploration. ArtX is a platform that empowers young artists to integrate art with multiple disciplines, shaping the future of artistic practice.

The gallery offers immersive experiences that highlight the evolving relationship between art and technology. It invites audiences to explore a vibrant visual world and engage with innovative artistic ideas.
